FAQ

Are you hiring or looking for volunteers?

We are not currently hiring for any paid or volunteer positions, but please keep an eye on our social channels and sign up for updates. We’ll keep you posted if anything changes.

How do I apply for a grant?

We are not accepting unsolicited grant proposals at this time.

Can I apply for the scholarship?

The scholarship is currently only open to Howard University students. Recipients are hand-picked by the university’s administration with a focus on students who exemplify Chadwick’s values, showcase exceptional skills in the arts and demonstrate financial need.

To learn more about the scholarship, head to theatrearts.howard.edu.

I’m a content creator/filmmaker/artist/student. Can I create some content for you?

Thank you for thinking of us to share your talents. We’re not currently able to take on something like this, but please keep an eye on our website and on socials to stay informed if anything changes.

Are donations tax deductible?

Yes, donations are tax deductible.
