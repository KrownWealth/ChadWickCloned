The Chadwick Boseman Foundation for the Arts (CBFA) aims to continue Chadwick Boseman’s legacy and support the intellectual, spiritual and artistic growth of people of the African Diaspora. 

The Chadwick Boseman Foundation for the Arts (CBFA) aims to continue Chadwick Boseman’s legacy and support the intellectual, spiritual and artistic growth of people of the African Diaspora. 

Support the next generation

Through scholarships, targeted gifts and grants, and lifelong mentorship and support, CBFA will work with artists across the globe to continue Chadwick’s work of inspiring and uplifting through the arts.
