Description of the Services Website

CBFA aims to continue Chadwick Boseman’s legacy of uplifting and inspiring through the arts. CBFA activities include developing a program of annual and at-need scholarships for students at Historically Black Colleges and Universities (HBCU) studying the arts, the administering of targeted impact gifts and grants on a case-by-case basis to community theater and arts programs in Black communities, and ongoing programming for young artists to provide continued support and mentorship throughout their artistic careers. The Website provides users with access to a variety of information, resources, works of authorship, e-newsletters, videos, and other relevant content (collectively, the “Content”).
